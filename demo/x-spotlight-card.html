Web Component · Visual Effect

x-spotlight-card

A card surface with a soft radial spotlight that follows the cursor. Decorative — does not change keyboard or click semantics. Respects prefers-reduced-motion by falling back to a centred, static spotlight.

Variants

Two surface treatments: an elevated drop-shadow card and a flat bordered card.

Elevated · primary

Shadowed surface with a blue spotlight. Hover to follow.

Bordered · primary

Flat surface with a 1px border.

Live controls

Live preview

Move your cursor across this card to see the spotlight follow. Toggle static to keep it centred.

Tip: enable “Reduce motion” in your OS settings — the spotlight will stay centred without pointer tracking, matching the static flag.
