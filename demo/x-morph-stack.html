Two orbs (multi-element goo merge)

Two orbs with their own data-morph-ids morph between apart (200 px between centres) and together (heavily overlapping). The gooey filter only shows its signature blob-merging effect when two filtered ghosts are close enough for their halos to overlap — so this is the demo where liquid actually looks like liquid. Try the same morph in clean (no goo, just two crisp circles), organic (mild bridge, plus visible bounce around the target), and liquid (the orbs visibly bridge into one blob mid-flight).

Reset overrides

The variant selector applies to all three demos. The sliders override the variant's spring values; click Reset overrides to fall back to the variant defaults. Mass slider is /10 (so 10 → 1.0). The duration slider at 0 means auto (use the spring's natural time); any other value stretches the spring to that many milliseconds while keeping its character. Demo 2 (button → modal) starts in liquid.
