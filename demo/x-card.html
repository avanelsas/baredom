Web Component · Light & Dark Mode

x-card

Themeable surface container for grouping related content. This demo follows your system light and dark mode setting and uses a more polished, professional surface palette.

Surface variants

The card supports elevated, outlined, filled, and ghost treatments while keeping a consistent content structure.

Elevated

Default

Soft elevation for content that should feel distinct from the page.

Outlined

Structured

Clear edge definition for forms, settings panels, and grouped metadata.

Filled

Emphasis

A stronger surface treatment for highlighted summaries and dashboard modules.

Ghost

Minimal

Minimal chrome for low-emphasis containers embedded in richer layouts.

Padding and radius

Spacing and corner radius can be tuned without changing the content model.

Compact card

Small padding and subtle rounding for dense layouts.

Spacious card

Larger padding and a softer silhouette for hero content.
