Web Component · Effect

x-confetti

A celebration effect: imperative fire() bursts of paper-confetti. Stateless config, event-shaped trigger, and full prefers-reduced-motion support.

Overlay (full-page)

One x-confetti with mode="overlay" sits at fixed/inset:0 across the viewport. Click to fire from the top.

Inline (within a stage)

mode="inline" confines particles to the host's bounding box. Click anywhere inside the stage to fire from that point.

Color & shape overrides

Pass per-burst overrides into fire(opts).

Sandbox

Tweak the live attributes on the overlay element, then fire. Each control sets the matching HTML attribute (or property) on <x-confetti id="overlay">.
